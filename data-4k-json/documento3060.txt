Alternativas:
A. 2, 1, 0, 8, 7, 5, 10
B. 2, 1, 0, 10, 5, 7, 8
C. 0, 1, 8, 7, 5, 10, 2
D. 0, 1, 2, 8, 7, 5, 10
E. 0, 1, 2, 10, 5, 7, 8
